Being A Fashion Designer

Fashion Design and the Passion for Fashion

The Fashion Industry has be running for decades, centuries even. But what exactly does it take to be a fashion designer? Let's talk about some of the things that goes into fashion designing.

Fashion designers create clothing, sketch designs, select fabrics, and patterns, and give instructions on how to make the things they design.
There are different types of fashion designers including, accessory designers, costume/clothing designers, and shoe designers. They can work for brands and fashion houses, in the film and television industry or on their own.

Here are some skills you need to be a company's or self designer!

Artistic Ability: Sketch your own design ideas and prototypes! Be able to express your vision through drawing and illustration.

Communication skills:Often working in teams throught projects, you must have effective communication skills.

Computer skills: CAD or Computer Aided Design programs are often used for graphic editing softwares, being efficent in computer skills will help you further extend your creativity!

Creativity: Unique, stylish, and proficient, your design must speak and shine through a variety of shapes, shades, and linens!

Decision-Making skills: You are always exposed to many ideas, therefore you must be able to decide which suggestions to include inside your design!

Detail Oriented: Have a good eye for the smaller things in fashion. Chains, holes, and patterns all make a difference in design.
